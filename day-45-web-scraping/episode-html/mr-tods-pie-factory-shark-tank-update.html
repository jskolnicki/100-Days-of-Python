Mr. Tod’s Shark Tank Tale: Sweet Potato Pie Factory

April 30, 2022March 31, 2022

In Season 1 Episode 1, Tod Wilson appeared on Shark Tank seeking $460,000 for 10% of his sweet potato pie factory, Mr. Tod’s Sweet Potato Pies.

Here’s a quick recap before we dive into Mr. Tod’s Shark Tank update.

Where Are They Now: Is Mr. Tods Pies Still in Business Today?

In 2017, Tod closed down his retail store in Englewood, New Jersey, and shifted his focus to selling direct to consumer, though a few stores in New Jersey still stock his pies for sale.

2022 Update: Mr. Tods Pies is still in business an currently selling its signature pies online and at its Paterson, New Jersey-based bakery 1.. Tod Wilson remains as CEO of the the Southern-style sweet potato pie factory where he works alongside his wife and celebrity private Chef, Danielle Saunders 2
